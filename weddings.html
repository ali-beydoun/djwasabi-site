Common Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For peak wedding season (September-May), we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. Popular Saturday dates fill quickly!

Do you take song requests from guests?

Absolutely! I'm happy to take requests that fit your celebration's vibe. You can also provide a do-not-play list.

Can you provide ceremony music?

Yes! I can cover your ceremony with processional, signing, and recessional music using a separate wireless setup.

Do you have backup equipment?

Always! I bring backup speakers, microphones, and music players to ensure your celebration continues smoothly.

Can you accommodate cultural music?

Yes! I specialize in multicultural weddings with music spanning Lebanese, Greek, Italian, Spanish, Asian, and Australian.

What's included in MC services?

All announcements, introductions, coordinating with vendors, running the schedule, and keeping your reception flowing perfectly.
