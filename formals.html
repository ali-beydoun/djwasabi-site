School Event FAQs

Do you have a Working With Children Check?

Yes, I have a current NSW Working With Children Check (WWCC) and can provide verification to schools upon request.

Can you provide clean versions of songs?

Absolutely! I maintain an extensive library of radio-edit and clean versions of all popular songs suitable for school events.

Do you work with school committees?

Yes! I regularly collaborate with student committees, teachers, and P&C groups to ensure the event meets everyone's expectations.

What about inappropriate song requests?

I'm experienced in handling requests diplomatically, redirecting to appropriate alternatives while keeping students happy.

Can you manage formal proceedings?

Yes! I can MC all formal aspects including arrivals, group photos, speeches, and awards while maintaining the appropriate atmosphere.

Do you offer school discounts?

I offer special rates for schools and can work within budget constraints. Multiple event bookings receive additional considerations.
