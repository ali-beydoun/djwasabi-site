Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 February 2025

1. Who We Are

DJ Wasabi is operated by Wassim ("we", "us", "our"). We provide professional DJ and MC services across Sydney, Australia. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or contact us.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Event details (type, date, venue, guest count)
  • Any additional information you provide in your message

We also automatically collect anonymised usage data through Google Analytics (see Section 5).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Provide a quote for your event
  • Communicate about event planning and details
  • Improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • FormSubmit.co — processes contact form submissions and delivers them to our email. Your form data passes through their servers. FormSubmit Privacy
  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — collects anonymised website usage data to help us understand how visitors use our site. Google Privacy Policy

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies set by Google Analytics to collect anonymised usage data. These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. They do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your contact form data for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and provide our services. If you book an event with us, we retain your information for the duration of our business relationship and a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at djwasabi1@outlook.com.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Our website is served over HTTPS to encrypt data in transit.

9. Australian Privacy Act

This privacy policy is governed by the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). While DJ Wasabi may not be required to comply with all APPs as a small business, we are committed to handling your information responsibly and transparently.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us:
