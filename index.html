About Wassim

Hey there! I'm Wassim, also known as DJ Wasabi. With over 5 years of experience as a professional DJ, I've had the incredible opportunity to bring energy, emotion, and music to every celebration.

My massive music library covers everything from R&B and Arabic music to Reggaetón, Afrobeats, throwbacks, and the latest hits. I pride myself on being adaptable and personalised to fit each event's unique vibe.

I understand that every event is unique, and I'm committed to working closely with you to understand your vision and bring it to life through music and entertainment.
