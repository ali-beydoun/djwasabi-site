Karaoke FAQs

What if I can't find my song?

With thousands of songs in the library, we likely have it! I also take requests in advance and can add specific songs for your event.

Do you have songs in different languages?

Yes! The library includes songs in English, Arabic, Spanish, Italian, and many other languages.

Can we have both karaoke and dancing?

Absolutely! I seamlessly blend karaoke sessions with DJ sets to keep everyone entertained.

How many microphones do you provide?

I bring 2-6 wireless microphones depending on your package, perfect for solos, duets, or group performances.

Do you help shy singers?

Of course! I create a supportive atmosphere and can even join in for moral support if needed.

Can we record performances?

While I don't provide recording services, you're welcome to record videos for memories!
